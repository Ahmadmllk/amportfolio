Data Analysis & Reporting

 Conducting thorough market research and data analysis is a critical component of my work. This section of my portfolio showcases some of the key market research and data analysis reports I have produced to support effective marketing strategies and data-driven decision making. 

  • Drone Advertising

    Overview: An entrepreneur wants to launch a startup that offers advertising spots to businesses via drones
    Goals:

    • Analyze the opportunity represented by the use of drones in advertising and marketing.
    • Examine the different ways of using drones in advertising: advertising clips, drone delivery, flying advertising (drone-vertising).
    • Evaluate the effectiveness and profitability of the use of advertising drones in terms of visibility and production costs.

    View Report

  • Mena Tours

    Overview: A travel agency wants to expand its offering to new customer segments this market research aims to analyze the travel agency sector, including its competitive landscape, market trends, and the impact of various factors on its growth.
    Goals:

    • Identify the major players in the market.
    • Analyze the impact of government policies, economic conditions, and technological advancements on the sector.
    • Examine the barriers that new entrants face in the market.

    View Report

  • The Banks Customer Relations Podium

    Overview: The Customer Relations Podium rewards and highlights the banks that best satisfy their customers and users and reveals the actions implemented to achieve this.
    Goals:

    • Evaluate customer awareness, usage and interest in banks in the sector.
    • Evaluate customer satisfaction with banking products and services, and Tunisians' perception of the latest innovations.
