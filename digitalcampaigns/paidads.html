Paid Ads

Platforms: Facebook, Instagram

Campaign Overview

This paid Facebook campaign aims to promote the company’s Montessori products as a tool that helps toddlers with pronunciation and cognitive development. By using organic & paid UGC, the campaign aims to generate curiosity among problem aware audience, increase brand awareness, and establish the brand as a go-to resource for educational toys that help with toddlers development.

Key Objectives

Increase Website Traffic: By using mainly educational videos to promote the products, the campaign aims to attract parents and grandparents to click on the link and visit the landing page to explore the product features & benefits, where they will enter a user journey based on their on-site behavior.

Engage Targeted Audience: The campaign aims to engage the targeted audience of parents, grandparents, and other 40+ years old individuals. By creating compelling content and encouraging interaction, the objective is to foster engagement with the landing page, such as subscribing to email list, add to cart, initiate checkout and purchases. Additionally, help build relationships and establish a connection with the audience, further enhancing brand perception and trust.

Results

Total Impressions 377,650

Total Clicks 21,982

Avg. Click-Through Rate​ 5,82%

Avg. Cost-per-click $0.55

Total Spend $12,000

Add To Cart 11%

Purchases 4%
